LinkedIn for Democrats

Time to get organized!

The Problem

Every two years national elections take place throughout the country but as American mobility has increased - with many people living in multiple cities and states throughout their lives - we do not have an accurate idea of what elections might particularly matter to or resonate with them.

Our Solution

Build a LinkedIn for Democrats.

Through a LinkedIn-like database, we (the Democratic Party) can better know who are people are and what (where) they care about!

Not only would this be an unprecedented tool for activating our base and for fundraising, but we will also better match potential volunteers with tasks that meet their skill sets.

We don’t want former Senate Communications Directors answering phones on Congressional campaigns and we don’t want CEOs on DCCC spam lists.

Let’s get organized and get better!
